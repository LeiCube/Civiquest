Welcome to CiviQuest

CiviQuest is a gamified mobile app that teaches civic education and fact-checking through real-world scenarios and storytelling. Join us in shaping critical thinkers of tomorrow!

What is CiviQuest?

CiviQuest is an interactive, story-driven game that helps young Filipinos to become truth-seekers in a digital age filled with misinformation. Set in the fictional town of Veritas, players take on the role of a journalist investigating a local mystery tied to a political scandal. Along the way, they’ll use real-world fact-checking tools, navigate social media platforms, and uncover the truth—one clue at a time. It’s like a story, puzzle, and adventure—all in one game!

Partnership with VERA Files

We’ve teamed up with VERA Files, a pioneer in fact-checking and media literacy in the Philippines. Through their expertise, we gained a deeper understanding of how misinformation spreads and how to responsibly verify online content. VERA Files generously shared their knowledge and introduced us to professional fact-checking tools and methods. Their guidance played a key role in shaping CiviQuest, allowing us to integrate realistic and educational fact-checking features into the game.

We are deeply grateful for their support as mentors and collaborators. Together, we aim to empower a new generation of critical thinkers who can confidently distinguish fact from fiction in the digital world.

Our Goal

CiviQuest was created with four main goals in mind:

Promote Media Literacy
Equip players with tools to critically evaluate online content and social media posts.

Encourage Civic Engagement
Inspire young people to get involved in community issues and advocate for truth.

Make Learning Interactive
Use game-based storytelling to make complex topics like disinformation fun and accessible.

Build a Culture of Verification
Normalize fact-checking as part of everyday online behavior.
